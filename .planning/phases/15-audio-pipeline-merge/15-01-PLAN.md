Rationale per Phase 7 §4.9 + PITFALLS §2.6: "5-task compression; consistency context unified". **spatial_audio disposition decision (D-1):** Fold spatial_audio into audio_pipeline as a 6th sub-step rather than deprecate it. Rationale: spatial audio rendering (Dolby Atmos bed+objects, HRTF binaural, 5 immersive sound patterns) is fundamentally a mixer/mastering concern — it operates on the same stems mixer operates on, just adds spatial encoding. Keeping it as a distinct sub-step preserves its unique technical content (Atmos metadata, HRTF tables, binaural panning) while unifying the consistency context. Old `spatial_audio/SKILL.md` becomes a redirect stub with `status: folded_into` (distinct from `merged_into` to record the disposition explicitly). Per ROADMAP §15 criterion #2 this choice MUST be documented in the SKILL.md body.
