<!-- HOOK taxonomy (CONTEXT D-2 — 5 types, fixed, do not invent more). -->

5 hook types:
  - 情感钩 (emotional hook)
  - 悬念钩 (suspense hook)
  - 冲突钩 (conflict hook)
  - 反差钩 (contrast hook)
  - 情绪爆点钩 (emotional-peak hook)

<!-- 3-second frame-by-frame structure (CONTEXT D-2). -->

Each of 15 examples MUST have:
  - 0-1s: Attention-grab — visual/audio jolt (sudden movement, loud sound, shocking image)
  - 1-2s: Context-establish — who / where / what's-at-stake in 1 sentence equivalent
  - 2-3s: Hook-pin — explicit promise of payoff ("you won't believe what happens next")

<!-- 5-tier strength scoring (CONTEXT D-2). -->

Tier rubric (fixed symbols):
  - 🎯 bullseye — instantly hooks; ≥ 85% predicted 完播率 hold
  - ✅ strong — hooks most viewers; 60-85% predicted hold
  - ⚠️ weak — slow burn; 30-60% predicted hold; needs pre-roll
  - ❌ broken — loses viewers; < 30% predicted hold
  - 💀 anti-hook — actively drives skip (e.g., cold logo, long silence)
