Apply this template to all 16 nodes. Specific content guidance per node:

**For `creative_source`** (root, AI-bounded):
- `aigc_transformation: ai_bounded` — AI assists but cannot replace
- `success_criteria`: "kernel novelty score ≥ 0.7 (measured against trope catalog embedding); creator acceptance = pass/fail"
- `fail_modes`: "LLM defaults to cliché tropes from training data; kernel lacks specificity from lived experience"
- `fallback_strategy`: "human creator provides seed anecdote; LLM expands rather than originates"
- `cost_budget`: low yuan (human-time-heavy, low compute); cost_driver: human_time
- `model_horizon: stable_2026` (creative intent origination is human; AI role is consistent)

**For `screenplay`** (narrative, AI-accelerated):
- `aigc_transformation: full_generation` — AI generates from creative_source kernel
- `success_criteria`: "script_auditor.score ≥ 0.75 across 5 dimensions; Field 3-act structure compliance = 100%"
- `fail_modes`: "plot holes; character-network inconsistencies; dialogue-naturalness < threshold; pace-flat mid-Act II"
- `cost_budget`: medium yuan (LLM call + iteration with script_auditor)
- `model_horizon: evolving`

**For `script_auditor`** (critic):
- `aigc_transformation: verification` — AI critiques
- `success_criteria`: "Pearson correlation with human reviewers ≥ 0.65 (v1 validated); audit_complete = true"
- `cost_budget`: low yuan (single LLM call per audit iteration)
- `model_horizon: stable_2026` (LLM-as-judge pattern is stable)

**For `visual_executor`** (drawer + animator):
- `aigc_transformation: full_generation` — AI generates images + video
- `success_criteria`: "identity_match ≥ 0.85 across shots (continuity_auditor metric); aesthetic_alignment ≥ 0.7 vs style_genome"
- `fail_modes`: "identity drift; style drift; composition drift; temporal flicker in video"
- `fallback_strategy`: "regenerate with stricter ControlNet; if N retries fail, human sketch + img2img"
- `cost_budget`: HIGH yuan (most expensive node — image + video generation)
- `model_horizon: evolving`

**For `audio_pipeline`** (5 audio tasks merged):
- `aigc_transformation: full_generation + augmentation` (mixed across sub-tasks)
- `success_criteria`: "LUFS within ±1 of platform spec; dialogue_intelligibility ≥ 0.9; lip_sync_offset ≤ 80ms"
- `fail_modes`: "TTS pronunciation degradation on Chinese dialects; music_emotion mismatch; LUFS out-of-spec"
- `cost_budget`: medium yuan (5 sub-tasks but each relatively cheap)

**For `theory_critic`** (consultative):
- `aigc_transformation: ai_bounded + verification` (AI surfaces theoretical concerns; human decides)
- `trigger_mode: creator_pulled` (META-06)
- `success_criteria`: "consultation invoked = true/false (no quantitative score); creator reports consultation useful = Likert ≥ 4/5"
- `cost_budget`: minimal yuan (only invoked when creator pulls)

(Continue similar pattern for remaining 10 nodes — fill content per Phase 7 §4 derivation + STACK/PITFALLS guidance.)
