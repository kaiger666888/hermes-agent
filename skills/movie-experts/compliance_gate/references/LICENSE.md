## No third-party copyrighted creative material

This corpus does NOT embed:
- Screenplay excerpts from copyrighted 短剧 / 微电影 (only pattern abstractions)
- Audio / video frames from copyrighted works
- Full-text reproductions of any copyrighted book / paper (only statute citations + thresholds)

If a future ref needs to cite a creator-licensed sample exceeding fair-use limits (e.g. > 30 seconds of video dialogue), that ref MUST add a dedicated `LICENSE.md` entry above naming the rights holder and the license grant.
