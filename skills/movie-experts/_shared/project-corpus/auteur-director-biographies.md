## 1. Ingmar Bergman — Dream Methodology

### From 伯格曼论电影 (Project -007)

**Book structure (verified):**
- **梦/梦想者** (Dream/Dreamer) — core metaphor
- **初期的电影**: 插科打诨/小丑/无赖行为/信任
- **其他的电影**: 闹剧/嬉闹
- Per-film diaries: *Wild Strawberries*, *Hour of the Wolf*, *Persona*, *Cries and Whispers*, *The Silence*
- Date-stamped entries: "July 26", "August 21", etc.

### Bergman's methodology
- Treat films as dreams to be analyzed
- Keep a creation diary for each film
- Self-analysis precedes external analysis

### Style fingerprint
- B&W chamber drama
- Religious doubt / silence of God
- Female psychologies
- Face close-ups (cf. Bergman → Bergman-style in 短剧 = extreme close-up)

## 2. Krzysztof Kieślowski — Documentary Roots

### From 基耶斯洛夫斯基谈基耶斯洛夫斯基 (Project -030)

**Book structure (verified):**
- **回家** (Home) — childhood
- **电影学校** (Film School)
- **纪录片的独特角色** (Documentary's unique role)
- Per-film analysis: *First Love*, *Curriculum Vitae*, *Hospital*, *I Don't Know*, *Night Porter*, *Ten Commandments*, *Veronika*, *Three Colors*

### Kieślowski's methodology
- Started in documentary ("documentary education")
- Concern with **contingency and fate** — the central Kieślowski motif
- Three Colors (Blue/White/Red = Liberty/Equality/Fraternity)

### Style fingerprint
- Chance encounters
- Intersecting lives
- Color coding (Blue/White/Red literally)
- Tonal melancholy

## 3. Andrei Tarkovsky — Spiritual Cinema

### From 七部半 (Project -038)

**Book structure (verified):**
- **Part 1 Growth**: Enlightenment, autobiography, *The Steamroller and the Violin*
- **Part 2A Innovation**: *Ivan's Childhood* (Sartre commentary), *Andrei Rublev*, *Solaris*, *The Mirror*
- **Part 2B Quest**: *Stalker*'s "Zone", *Nostalghia*, *The Sacrifice*

### Tarkovsky's methodology
- "Seven and a half" = 7 features + 1 short
- Sculpting time, poetic logic, faith & art
- Anti-formalist: structure from spiritual necessity

See also: [`film-philosophy-bazin-tarkovsky.md`](./film-philosophy-bazin-tarkovsky.md)

## 4. Yasujirō Ozu — Tatami Perspective

### From 小津安二郎的艺术 (Project -057)

**Book structure (verified):**
- **小津作品的风格** (Style of Ozu's work):
  - **仰拍** (Low-angle shot) — Ozu's signature
  - 人物安排成相似形 (Characters arranged in similar shapes)
  - 从正面拍摄人物 (Shoot characters from front)
  - 速度、类似与反复 (Speed, similarity, repetition)
- **学艺时期** → **蒲田-大船风格的形成**
- **美国电影的影响** (Lubitsch influence)
- **"喜八片"的世界** ("Kihachi films" world)
- **崩溃的感觉** (Sense of collapse) — *The Only Son* onward

### Ozu's methodology
- Low-angle camera at tatami height
- Cross-scene similar composition
- Recurring motifs (clothes, empty shots)
- **No camera movement** in classical period

### Style fingerprint
- Family drama
- Generational tension
- Still life transitions
- Elliptical storytelling

## 5. Rainer Werner Fassbinder — Repertory Brutalism

### From 法斯宾德的世界 (Project -013)

**Book structure (verified):**
- **Chapter 1 Lost Childhood** (失落的童年): Androgynous psyche
- **Chapter 2 Group & Workshop** (团体与工作组):
  - Drama school
  - Two short films
  - **Action Theater** (行动剧场)
  - Anti-Theater (反剧场)
  - Magnetic field, Bocula
- Prolific output: 40+ films, died at 37

### Fassbinder's methodology
- Theater → film conversion
- Workshop model (fixed repertory cast)
- Themes: homosexuality, immigrants, women, melodrama

### Style fingerprint
- Fassbinder troupe (固定 cast)
- Sirkian melodrama influence
- Political brutality
- Mirrors and frames

## 6. Luis Buñuel — Surrealist to Mexican

### From 我的最后一口气 (Project -054)

**Book structure (verified, autobiography):**
- Memory, childhood recollections, Calanda drums
- Zaragoza home, Conchita memories
- Madrid 1917-1925
- Paris 1925-1929
- **Dreams & Fantasies**
- **Surrealism 1929-1933**
- America, Hollywood
- Mexico 1946-1961

### Buñuel's methodology
- Surrealism → Mexican period → French late period
- Dreams, desire, religious critique
- Recurring imagery (insects, feet, Catholic iconography)

### Style fingerprint
- Surrealist set-pieces
- Anti-clerical satire
- Class critique
- Dream logic

## 7. Robert Altman — Anti-Hollywood Ensemble

### From 不要给我讲故事，我需要的是人物 (Project 075)

**Book core proposition (verified):**
> "Don't tell me a story, I need characters" (不要给我讲故事，我需要的是人物)

### Altman's methodology
- Anti-Hollywood narrative
- Ensemble films (*Nashville*, *The Player*, *Gosford Park*)
- Overlapping dialogue
- Genre subversion (*McCabe & Mrs. Miller*, *The Long Goodbye*)

### Style fingerprint
- Multi-character weave
- Overlapping audio
- Long takes with zoom
- Genre deconstruction

## Auteur Research Method (synthesized from 7 books)

### 5 Research Dimensions

1. **Growth trajectory** — Childhood / family / education / film school
2. **Workshop & interpersonal** — Fixed cast / actor partners / production collaborators
3. **Thematic evolution** — Motif progression from early to late
4. **Style fingerprint** — Inimitable visual / narrative signatures
5. **Historical context** — Author vs era (New Hollywood / French New Wave / German New Cinema)

### Source Material Types

| Material | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Creation diary** | Internal view | Bergman diaries |
| **Interview record** | Self-explanation | Kieślowski, Tarkovsky |
| **Theoretical self-statement** | Systematized | Tarkovsky *Sculpting Time* |
| **Third-party bio** | External view | Fassbinder, Altman |
| **Memoir** | Retrospective | Buñuel *My Last Breath* |

## Application to AI 短剧

### Style genome 5D encoding
For each 短剧 IP, identify the "virtual auteur":
- Pick 1-2 reference directors from this list
- Encode their 5D signature in `style_genome`
- Use as constraint for `screenplay` / `cinematographer` / `editor`

### Example: 战神归来 短剧
- Virtual auteur: Altman (ensemble) + Hitchcock (gaze)
- 5D blend: density 7, formality 3, color 4, motion 6, sound 8
- Diagnostic: too Altman for runtime → reduce ensemble size

### Example: 甜宠 短剧

- 5D blend: density 4, formality 5, color 7, motion 3, sound 5
- Diagnostic: too Ozu → increase dialogue density
