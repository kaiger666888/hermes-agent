## 2. 《如何写影评》8th ed. (Project 004-02) — Operational Manual

### Book core tools (verified)
- **自问十题** ("Ask Yourself 10 Questions") — core method
- **影评该怎样** ("How should a film review be") — operational guide
- 8th edition additions: digital era, new media criticism

### The Self-Ask 10 Questions

Before writing, answer each:
1. What is the relationship between title and story?
2. What is the relationship between genre and story?
3. What is the relationship between technique and story?
4. Who is the target audience?
5. What is the film's commercial vs artistic positioning?
6. How does the film reflect director's style?
7. What symbols / metaphors appear?
8. How does the film relate to its era?
9. What is the strongest emotional moment?
10. What are the film's weaknesses?

## 4 Criticism Dimensions (synthesized from 3 books)

### Dimension 1: Audiovisual Language Analysis (Dai Ch.1)
- **Frame**: composition, symmetry, space
- **Shot scale**: wide / full / medium / close / extreme close-up
- **Movement**: camera / subject
- **Lighting**: high/low key, light-shadow contrast
- **Color**: primary / secondary / color arc
- **Sound**: dialogue / music / SFX

### Dimension 2: Narrative Analysis (Metz's syntagm theory)
- **Grand syntagms**:
  - Autonomous shot
  - Parallel syntagm
  - Bracket syntagm
  - Descriptive syntagm
  - Narrative syntagm
  - Alternate narrative syntagm
  - Scene
  - Episodic sequence
- This is Metz's classical classification

### Dimension 3: Auteur Theory (Dai Ch.2)
- Director's motifs (recurring elements)
- Director's style fingerprint
- Cross-work evolution
- Comparison with other auteurs

### Dimension 4: Cultural Studies
- Gender / class / race
- Ideology
- Post-colonial, queer theory
- Historical context

## Critical Writing Structure (best practice)

### Academic criticism structure
```
1. Introduction: film basic info + central thesis
2. Audiovisual analysis: select 1-2 key scenes
3. Narrative analysis: structure, theme, characters
4. Auteur theory: relation to director's other works
5. Cultural context: era, region, industry
6. Conclusion: integrated evaluation
```

### Media criticism structure (《如何写影评》)
```
1. One-sentence verdict (recommend/not + reason)
2. Plot summary (≤200 chars)
3. Strengths/weaknesses analysis (2-3 points)
4. Suitable audience
5. Final verdict
```

## Application to AI 短剧

### Critical framework for 短剧
Most 短剧 criticism is level 1-2 (impressionistic / descriptive). To reach level 3-5:

1. **Audiovisual analysis**: Analyze shot scale / cutting rate / color temperature
2. **Narrative analysis**: Identify beat structure (Snyder 15-beat)
3. **Auteur analysis**: Compare to original IP's authorship
4. **Cultural context**: Platform algorithm, audience demographics

### 《如何写影评》"Self-Ask 10" applied to 短剧
1. Title — does it communicate the hook?
2. Genre — 战神 vs 甜宠 vs 校园?
3. Technique — AI-generated vs live-action?
4. Audience — 男频 / 女频 / 中年?
5. Positioning — 抖音 / 快手 / 小程序剧?
6. Style — recurring director DNA?
7. Symbols — status markers (car, watch, clothing)?
8. Era — when does it take place?
9. Emotional peak — 爽点 location?
10. Weaknesses — what's filler?

This 10-question protocol is the **most actionable** export of academic film criticism to 短剧 production.
