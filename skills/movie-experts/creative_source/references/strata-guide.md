# Six-Strata Guide (六层地层学)

**Source:** Adapted from OpenClaw kais-soul-radar strata-guide.md + Bourdieu field theory + Giddens structuration theory + Barthes narrative analysis + Foucault power/knowledge doctrine.
**Copyright:** Fair Use — methodology distillation.
**Last-verified:** 2026-06-16

## Summary

The authoritative framework for the 6 social strata layers used by creative_source. Each layer defines: scope, theoretical basis, core analysis dimensions, recommended data sources (with URLs), and a canonical story kernel example. Together, the 6 layers form a complete toolkit for mining structural narratives from social reality.

## Heuristics

### Layer L1 — 制度地层 / Institutional stratum

**Scope:** 法律、政策、制度文本中的权力分配逻辑——谁被规训、谁被豁免、什么行为被赋予价值、什么行为被剥夺意义。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **Foucault 权力/知识理论** — institutions exercise power by defining "normal"
- **Bourdieu 场域理论** — capital distribution in institutional fields determines actor positions
- **Marxist superstructure analysis** — law and policy reflect ruling class interests

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 规训对象 — which populations/behaviors are重点 regulated?
- 豁免区域 — which powers/interests are exempt from regulation?
- 话语控制 — how does the institution define "problem" and "solution"?
- 规训转移 — how are collective risks transferred to individuals?

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 中国政府网政策文件 | https://www.gov.cn/zhengce/ | Latest policy full-text |
| 国务院公报 | https://www.gov.cn/gongbao/ | Authoritative policy archive |
| 最高人民法院裁判文书 | https://wenshu.court.gov.cn/ | Judicial practice |
| 全国人大法律法规 | http://www.npc.gov.cn/npc/c2/c3082/ | Legal full-text DB |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> 灵活就业政策将社保缴纳责任完全转移给个人,但平台经济的不稳定收入使得连续缴纳成为不可能,形成了"有选择权但每个选项都通向脆弱"的制度陷阱。

**不可言说性:** 7/10 (讨论灵活就业时,焦点常在"平台责任"而非"制度设计")

**Search keywords:** `政策调整 个人权益`, `灵活就业 社保`, `平台经济 监管`, `劳动法 判决`, `户籍 限制`, `行业整顿 从业人员`

---

### Layer L2 — 技术地层 / Technological stratum

**Scope:** 技术变革(AI、自动化、数字化)对人类劳动和生存位置的位移效应——谁被替代、谁被增强、谁被推向新的边缘。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **Braverman 劳动过程理论** — technological progress transfers skills from workers to machines
- **Rifkin 第三次工业革命** — structural technological unemployment trend
- **Acemoglu & Restrepo 任务模型** — automation replaces tasks but creates new ones

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 替代地图 — which professions/skills are being replaced? Speed?
- 增强不对等 — is tech enhancement evenly distributed?
- 技能贬值 — which experiences/qualifications are depreciating?
- 新型剥削 — has technology created new, more隐蔽 exploitation forms?

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| WEF Future of Jobs Report | https://www.weforum.org/reports/the-future-of-jobs-report/ | Global occupation replacement forecast |
| McKinsey 自动化研究 | https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-global-institute/ | Employment impact quantification |
| 中国信通院数字经济白皮书 | https://www.caict.ac.cn/ | China digital economy data |
| ILO 全球就业展望 | https://www.ilo.org/global/research/global-reports/ | International labor reports |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> AI 写作工具替代了初级文案岗位,但"擅长使用 AI 的人"恰好是最不该被替代的创意工作者——技术制造了一个悖论:最善于训练自己替代品的人,获得了最多的生存空间。

**不可言说性:** 6/10

**Search keywords:** `AI 替代 岗位`, `自动化 就业 冲击`, `大模型 职业影响`, `技术失业 再就业`, `AI 版权 原创`

---

### Layer L3 — 人口地层 / Demographic stratum

**Scope:** 人口结构变化(老龄化、少子化、人口流动)如何重塑社会基本参数——谁在增长、谁在消失、这些变化如何重新分配社会资源。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **人口转变理论 (Demographic Transition)** — inevitable path from high-birth-high-death to low-birth-low-death
- **推拉理论 (Push-Pull Theory)** — economic drivers of population flow
- **人口红利/负债** — age structure's structural impact on economic development

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 消失的一代 — which age groups/regions are shrinking? Consequences?
- 抚养比逆转 — how is the labor-to-dependent ratio changing?
- 流动的不对称 — who has the资格/capacity to move? Who is left behind?
- 服务缺口 — public service gaps created by demographic change

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 国家统计局人口普查 | https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/pcsj/rkpc/ | 7th national census data |
| UN Population Division | https://population.un.org/ | Global population forecast |
| 各省统计年鉴 | https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/tjgb/ | Provincial flow data |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> 农村劳动力向城市流动后,农村出现了"空心化"——留守老人独自承担农业生产和自我照护,而城市端他们建造的住房却因"人才引进"政策被优先分配给高学历年轻人。

**不可言说性:** 5/10

**Search keywords:** `老龄化 养老 压力`, `生育率 下降`, `人口流动 城市`, `少子化 教育`, `养老金 缺口`

---

### Layer L4 — 空间地层 / Spatial stratum

**Scope:** 居住空间的商品化进程——谁拥有空间、谁被挤出、空间如何成为阶层再生产的工具。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **Lefebvre 空间生产理论** — space is not neutral container but product of social relations
- **Harvey 空间修复理论** — capital realizes crisis transfer through real estate
- **Bourdieu 住宅资本** — housing as core tool for class reproduction

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 空间排斥 — which populations are systematically excluded from urban space?
- 阶层固化 — how does housing transmit economic advantage/disadvantage intergenerationally?
- 流动性陷阱 — how does high housing price lock social mobility?
- 空间正义 — is spatial distribution of public resources fair?

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 贝壳研究院住房报告 | https://www.ke.com/about/research | Housing market data |
| 国家统计局房价 | https://www.stats.gov.cn/ | Official housing stats |
| 各城市人才政策 | (municipal websites) | Talent introduction policies |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> 一线城市的"人才房"政策将新建保障房优先分配给硕博学历者,而实际建造这些住房的建筑工人(大多来自农村)无法在城市获得居住权——他们建造了自己无法居住的空间。

**不可言说性:** 6/10

**Search keywords:** `房价 城市`, `人才房 政策`, `住房 阶层`, `租房 压力`, `城镇化 边缘`

---

### Layer L5 — 代际契约地层 / Intergenerational-contract stratum

**Scope:** 父辈的语法在子辈世界是否还成立——代际之间的隐性契约(努力→回报、储蓄→安全、学历→阶层)如何失效。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **Mannheim 代际社会学** — generational units share historical consciousness
- **Beck 风险社会** — risk distribution has generational asymmetry
- **Bourdieu 文化资本代际传递** — cultural capital transfer is not frictionless

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 失效的承诺 — which父辈 advice no longer applies?
- 囤积 vs 断舍离 — material culture divergence between generations
- 婚育契约 — marriage/child-rearing expectations across generations
- 责任倒挂 — are子辈 bearing父辈 risks?

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 小红书代际话题 | https://www.xiaohongshu.com | Generational discourse |
| 知乎代际问题 | https://www.zhihu.com | Q&A on intergenerational conflict |
| 央视财经消费报告 | (CCTV financial) | Consumption divergence data |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> 父母囤积旧物的习惯源于匮乏时代的创伤记忆,但子辈在物质丰裕中长大,视囤积为心理负担——同一行为被两代人编码为完全相反的道德含义,但谁也不愿意承认对方的逻辑在自己世界里成立。

**不可言说性:** 4/10 (相对主流)

**Search keywords:** `代际消费`, `年轻人 父母`, `囤积 断舍离`, `婚育 焦虑`, `责任 父母`

---

### Layer L6 — 心灵地层 / Psychosocial stratum

**Scope:** 人的内在世界正在发生什么结构性坍缩——外部 L1-L5 力量作用后的内心沉积。

**Theoretical basis:**
- **Fromm 自由逃避** — modern freedom creates anxiety that drives escape into conformity
- **Erich Fromm 健全社会** — mental health as societal indicator
- **韩炳哲 疲倦社会** — achievement-subject self-exploitation
- **Lasch 自恋主义文化** — therapeutic culture's side effects

**Core analysis dimensions:**
- 意义危机 — what structural forces have collapsed meaning sources?
- 孤独的几何学 — how has loneliness been re-structured (networked vs isolated)?
- 身份焦虑 — what new identity anxieties has late modernity created?
- 心理殖民 — how has therapy/corporate language colonized inner life?

**Recommended data sources:**

| Source | URL | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 中国心理健康白皮书 | (中科院心理所) | Mental health stats |
| 社会心态蓝皮书 | (社科院) | Social mentality survey |
| 知乎心理话题 | https://www.zhihu.com | Public psychological discourse |
| 微博情绪研究 | (academic papers) | Aggregate sentiment data |

**Canonical story kernel example:**
> 年轻人普遍感到"间歇性疲惫+持续性焦虑",但找不到具体原因——这种弥漫感不是个人心理问题,而是结构性的:就业不稳定(L2)、住房不可及(L4)、社保缺失(L1)、代际期望冲突(L5)在心灵层的合力沉积。

**不可言说性:** 3/10 (主流安全,但讨论"结构性"需谨慎)

**Search keywords:** `心理健康 报告`, `孤独 调查`, `年轻人 意义感`, `焦虑 结构性`, `疲倦社会`

### Multi-strata overlay principles

**Single-layer stories are sharp but limited.** A pure L2 story (AI replaces copywriter) hits one note. Overlaying L2+L5 (AI replaces copywriter + parent generation doesn't understand why "good job" disappeared) creates resonance.

**The 3 canonical high-resonance overlays:**

| Overlay | Resonance | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| **L1 + L4** | 1.8x | 制度设计 + 空间分配 = 双重结构性锁定 |
| **L2 + L5** | 1.7x | 技术替代 + 代际期望冲突 = 时代断层 |
| **L3 + L6** | 1.6x | 人口变化 + 心灵沉积 = 沉默的代际创伤 |

**The 3-layer overlay (rare):**

| Overlay | Resonance | Why it works |
|---|---|---|
| **L1 + L2 + L4** | 2.5x | 制度+技术+空间三重锁定(最强故事核,但极难找到真实案例) |
